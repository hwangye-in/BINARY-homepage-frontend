Our History

Founded in a small dorm room in 2018, BINARY started as a study group for Introduction to Algorithms.

Today, we have grown into the largest technical student organization on campus, with over 200 active members and alumni working at top tech companies worldwide.

2018 - The Beginning

Founded by 5 CS students with a passion for algorithms.

2019 - First Hackathon

Hosted "BinaryHacks v1.0" with 50 participants.

2021 - Expansion

Launched mentorship program and industry partnership initiative.

2023 - Present Day

Recognized as "Student Organization of the Year". Launching new AI research division.
