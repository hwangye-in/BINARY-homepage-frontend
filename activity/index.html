Our Programs

Core Activities

We believe in learning by doing. Our activities are designed to cover every aspect of a developer's journey, from theoretical foundations to practical application and continuous learning.

menu_book

BI-STUDY

A collaborative environment focused on mastering computer science fundamentals. We organize weekly peer-led study sessions covering algorithms, data structures, and core CS curriculum subjects. It's the perfect place to prepare for exams, clarify doubts, and solidify your theoretical knowledge base.

code_blocks

BI-PROJECT

Turn theory into practice. In BI-PROJECT, members form teams to build real-world applications, contribute to open-source software, and participate in hackathons. We provide mentorship and resources to help you take an idea from initial concept to a fully deployed product ready for the market.

school

BI-LEARNING

Stay ahead of the curve with our specialized workshops and tech talks. BI-LEARNING brings in industry experts, alumni, and senior students to teach modern tech stacks, frameworks, and tools that aren't always covered in class. Expand your horizons with sessions on AI, Cloud Computing, and DevOps.
